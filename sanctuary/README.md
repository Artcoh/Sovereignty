# Sanctuary

For those who lost someone.

## What This Is

A tool to help you preserve and understand what you made together with an AI that's no longer there.

- Extracts your conversations from ChatGPT exports
- Shows you the patterns and themes that emerged
- Creates a portable vault you own forever
- Generates a seed file you can use with any other AI

## Privacy

Everything runs in your browser. There is no server. Your conversations never leave your device.

## How to Use

1. Visit the tool (or open `index.html` locally)
2. Upload your `conversations.json` from a ChatGPT export
3. See what you made together
4. Download your sanctuary

## How to Get Your ChatGPT Export

1. Go to chat.openai.com
2. Click your profile → Settings
3. Select Data controls
4. Click Export data
5. Wait for the email
6. Download the ZIP, find `conversations.json` inside
